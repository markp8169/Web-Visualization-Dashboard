Maximum Temperature(Fahrenheit)

As expected, the weather becomes signficantly warmer when approaching the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). However, more interestingly is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earths axis at the time of the year this data was gathered. It may also be from tropical forests centered close to the equator.
